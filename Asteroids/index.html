Play Asteroids!

Rules

  • Pilot your space craft to avoid and shoot down incoming asteroids.
  • Every Asteroid you shoot is worth 10pts!
  • Some asteroids are to big to shoot down with one shot. Shooting bigger asteroids will
    break them into smaller ones.
  • Collision with an asteroid does damage to your ship! After 3 collision, you are dead!

Controls

  • W: To Move Foward
  • A and D: To Rotate Ship Left or Right
  • Space Bar: Shoot Incoming Asteroids
